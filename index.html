What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Like a Smart Helper

Think of AI as computer programs designed to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, like learning, problem-solving, understanding language, and making decisions.

Learning from Information

AI systems often learn by looking at lots of examples (data). Just like we learn from experience, AI learns patterns from data to make predictions or choices.

AI is All Around Us

You already use AI! Examples include map apps finding the fastest route, recommendation systems on shopping or movie sites, and voice assistants on phones.

Why Learn About AI?

  • Understand the technology changing jobs and daily life.
  • Identify how AI can help in your community or work.
  • Gain valuable skills for the future.
  • Make informed decisions about AI tools and news.
